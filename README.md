## Breast Cancer Prediction
In this end-to-end ML project, we will predict whether a patient is likely to have breast cancer based on measurements taken on tumor cells present in an autopsy or medical imaging. 


## Description
In addition to the patient ID and the diagnosis, the dataset is formed of 30 features, all of float type, including radius, texture, perimeter, area, smoothness, and concavity. They will be then reduced to 5 features using various feature selection techniques including removal of features of low variance and removal of correlated features. 

Three ML classifiers, decision tree, random forest, and AdaBoost Classifier, will be built and based on the metrics, we will select the model that best predict the breast cancer occurrence.
